Burnaby Music Festival 2024

When the Burnaby Music Festival approached us with their vision for commemorative merchandise, we knew this project would require special attention to detail and creative problem-solving.

1

Challenge

Create vibrant, music-themed designs that would appeal to diverse age groups while maintaining festival branding.

2

Solution

Developed a gradient ink technique to achieve the sunset effect, using 4-color process printing with special metallic accents.

3

Result

Delivered 500 shirts in 5 days, with 98% customer satisfaction and requests for reorders for next year's festival.

Maria Santos

Festival Director

"Ink Creek transformed our vision into something even better than we imagined. The quality exceeded our expectations, and the team's creativity helped us create merchandise that festival-goers are still wearing months later."
