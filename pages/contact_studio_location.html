Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Standard orders: 5-7 business days. Rush orders: 1-3 business days (additional fees apply). Large orders (500+ pieces) may require 7-10 business days. We'll provide exact timelines when you submit your quote request.

Yes! We offer design consultation, artwork cleanup, and custom design creation. Basic design adjustments are included with orders. Complex custom designs start at $50. We can work with your existing artwork or create something completely new.

Our minimum order is 12 pieces for screen printing. For smaller quantities (1-11 pieces), we offer heat transfer vinyl or direct-to-garment printing. Digital printing options are available for orders under 12 pieces with different pricing.

Absolutely! We offer sample printing for $25-35 per piece (credited toward your final order). Visit our studio to see material samples, or we can create a sample with your design. This is especially recommended for orders over 100 pieces.

We prefer vector files (AI, EPS, PDF) for best quality. We also accept high-resolution PNG, JPG, and PSD files (300 DPI minimum). If you only have a low-resolution file, we can often recreate it as a vector for a small additional fee.
