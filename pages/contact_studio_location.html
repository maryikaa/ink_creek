Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

How quickly can you complete an order?

Standard turnaround is 7-10 business days. Rush orders (1-3 days) are available for an additional fee. Timeline depends on quantity, complexity, and current workload.

What's the minimum order quantity?

We don't have a strict minimum, but orders under 12 pieces may have higher per-unit costs due to setup fees. We're happy to work with you on small personal projects!

Do you provide design services?

Yes! Our in-house design team can help create or refine your artwork. We also offer design consultations to optimize your existing artwork for screen printing.

What file formats do you accept?

We prefer vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) for the best quality, but we can work with high-resolution PNG, PDF, or JPG files. We'll let you know if we need any adjustments.

Do you offer delivery in the Lower Mainland?

Yes, we offer delivery throughout the Lower Mainland for orders over $200. Pickup is always free, and we're conveniently located with free parking.
