Specialty Techniques

Advanced printing capabilities that set your apparel apart with unique textures, effects, and premium finishes.

Water-Based Inks

Soft-hand printing that absorbs into fabric fibers for a vintage feel and breathable finish. Perfect for fashion-forward designs.

3-5 business days

Discharge Printing

Removes dye from dark garments and replaces with color, creating ultra-soft prints that feel like part of the fabric.

5-7 business days

Metallic & Specialty Inks
